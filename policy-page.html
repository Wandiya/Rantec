What about Cookies?

  • Cookies are unique identifiers that we transfer to your device to enable our systems to recognize your device and to provide features to make your navigation experience unique and targeted.
  • You are not obligated to accept cookies when visiting the Site, however, it is important to note that without enabling cookies, you will be unable to use the basket function and place orders. Cookies are small pieces of text files that are stored on your computer's hard drive by your internet browser when you visit certain pages on the Site. They allow our server to recognize your computer as a unique user and can also identify your Internet Protocol address to save you time during your Site visit. We solely use cookies for your convenience and not for acquiring any other information about you or for targeted advertising purposes. Disabling cookie acceptance in your browser settings may limit your access to the Site. Rest assured that our usage of cookies does not contain any of your personal or private information and is free from viruses.
